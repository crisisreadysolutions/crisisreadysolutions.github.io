CPR/AED Training

Learn life-saving techniques with our comprehensive CPR and AED training program.

Our CPR/AED training follows the latest HSI/AHA guidelines and teaches participants how to recognize cardiac emergencies and properly perform CPR for adults, children, and infants. You'll also learn how to use an Automated External Defibrillator (AED) with confidence.

What You'll Learn:
  • How to recognize the signs of cardiac arrest
  • Proper CPR techniques for all age groups
  • AED operation and maintenance
  • Relief of choking
  • How to work as part of a resuscitation team

Duration: 2-3 hours

Certification: Valid for 2 years
