About Us

Learn about our mission and expertise

George Roat

Firefighter/Paramedic (Retired)

With over 30 years of experience in Fire/Emergency Medical Services, I founded Crisis Ready Solutions to bring professional CPR and First Aid training to Central Florida.

Throughout my career, I've witnessed firsthand the critical difference that proper emergency response training can make in saving lives. My mission is to equip individuals and organizations with the skills and confidence needed to respond effectively during medical emergencies.

As a retired firefighter/paramedic, I bring real-world experience and practical knowledge to every training session. I believe in hands-on, scenario-based learning that prepares you for actual emergency situations.
